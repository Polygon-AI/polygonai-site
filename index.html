Art as Code

Generative or algorithmic art is a relatively new format in which the artist-program​mer realizes a vision or idea by defining parameters and commands in a set of computer codes. It uses deep neural networks to replicate, recreate and blend styles of artwork, by teaching the AI to understand existing pieces of art.

Art ​of AI

AI Generated Art is a form of digital art that's created randomly with computer code with a central characteristic using autonomous machines and algorithms. Rules are created that provide boundaries for the creation process then a computer crunches those codes to produce the desired outcome.
